Essay on Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was the founder of Pakistan and one of the greatest leaders in history. He was born on 25 December 1876 in Karachi. He was a brilliant lawyer, a great politician, and a wise leader. Because of his hard work and determination, Muslims of the subcontinent got a separate homeland called Pakistan on 14 August 1947. Quaid-e-Azam believed in unity, faith, and discipline. He worked day and night for the rights of Muslims. He was an honest, brave, and intelligent person. People respected him because he always spoke the truth and fought for justice. After the creation of Pakistan, Quaid-e-Azam became the first Governor-General of the country. He wanted Pakistan to become a strong, peaceful, and successful nation.
Unfortunately, he died on 11 September 1948, but his services for Pakistan can never be forgotten. In conclusion, Quaid-e-Azam is our national hero. We should follow his principles and work hard for the progress of our country. Pakistanis are proud of him and will always remember his sacrifices.
